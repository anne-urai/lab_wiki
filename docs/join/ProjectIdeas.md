# Project ideas
Ideas for projects are listed and tracked [here](https://github.com/orgs/cocosys-lab/projects/2). For students, please first learn more about the ongoing projects of PhD students and postdocs to see who would be a suitable daily supervisor for your project.

Projects directly supervised by Anne should ideally move in the direction of social change and behavior for sustainability.
